Intro

As we approach the departure of 2024 batch, let's gather our collective gratitude and support to honor their remarkable contributions which have truly enriched our college experience, leaving a lasting impact on each of us.

This year , it's time for our department to host CHEERIO 2K24 , special farewell event for 4th year students.

Contribute

We kindly request each student to contribute Rs 500/- towards this farewell gesture. Your generosity will not only honor senior's efforts but also reinforce the spirit of unity within our department community. Together, let's create a farewell that reflects the depth of our appreciation and camaraderie.
