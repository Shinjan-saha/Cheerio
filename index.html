Intro

As we approach the departure of the 2025 batch, let's gather our collective gratitude and support to honor their remarkable contributions, which have truly enriched our college experience, leaving a lasting impact on each of us.

This year, it's time for our department to host CHEERIO 2K25, a special farewell event for 4th-year students.

Date: 3rd May
Time: 10:00AM to 4:30PM
Venue: Gseries

Contribute

We kindly request each student to contribute Rs 500/- towards this farewell gesture. Your generosity will not only honor senior's efforts but also reinforce the spirit of unity within our department community. Together, let's create a farewell that reflects the depth of our appreciation and camaraderie.
